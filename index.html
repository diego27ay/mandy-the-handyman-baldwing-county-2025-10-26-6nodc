About Mandy the Handyman

Mandy the Handyman was founded on a simple principle: every homeowner deserves access to honest, reliable, and professional handyman services. With over a decade of experience in home maintenance and repairs, Mandy started this business to serve the Fairhope and Daphne communities with the same level of care and attention that he brings to his own home. What began as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted name in local home services, built entirely on word-of-mouth recommendations and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to be the most trusted handyman service in Baldwin County by delivering exceptional craftsmanship, transparent communication, and competitive pricing. We believe that quality home maintenance shouldn't be complicated or expensive. We're committed to treating every home with respect, standing behind our work, and building lasting relationships with our customers. Whether you need a quick repair or a comprehensive home improvement project, we approach every job with the same dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction that has made us a local favorite.
